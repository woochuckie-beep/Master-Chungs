Welcome to Our Academy

Building Champions in Richmond Hill Since 2009

At Chung's Taekwondo Richmond Hill, we offer a variety of programs designed to meet the needs of our entire community. Located at 9625 Yonge Street Unit A, our academy provides high-quality martial arts instruction to kids, teens, and adults. With over 17 years of experience, we have become a cornerstone in promoting fitness, discipline, and camaraderie right here in Richmond Hill.

Come and experience the difference at Chung's Taekwondo Richmond Hill — where every student is part of a thriving, supportive community and every belt earned is a milestone celebrated.

Meet Master Chung

Master Chung
Head Master & Academy Owner

Born in Daegu, South Korea into a lineage of Taekwondo Masters, Master Chung has dedicated his life to the martial arts. With over 20 years of teaching experience and active training across Taekwondo, Kendo, and Boxing, he brings a depth of knowledge and passion to every class he leads.
